Viruses(AR), 1920 x 1080, Augmented Readlity, 24 sec, 2020

This project was created in March 2020, during the very beginning of the pandemic.

We never know what is virus looks like, and we can never see it through eyes.

Therefore we never know its infection.

An AR perspective of viewing viruses is a way to establish a sense of viruses that can be anywhere around us in daily life.

To keep people in mind that many viruses are extremely contagious, harmful, and dangerous.

We should never think it's never a big deal.







Virus Animation, 1920 x 1080, Digtal drawing and animation, 3 minutes 45 seconds, 2020

The animation is a series I am creating specifically for the period we live in now.

In this series, I am using both AfterEffect and Photoshop for the entire series due to my artist practice.

The content of this is to explore the emotion and reaction of people from everyday news reports I seem on the television and network.

Also, the perspective of people like me viewing this health crisis.

The reason I called it Animation 345 is because I want the length of this animation to be the actual day, which is 345 days.

Pretend when a disaster like the animation shows, or the crisis we live in now.

The duration of the crisis can end quickly, like the length of this video 345 days.

If countries from all over the world work together, accept each other's efforts in solving the crisis.

The crisis now and the crisis in the future will be less pain, less depressing, and less stressful for everyone compare to what happened up to this period.
