Final documentation

For this final phase, we are confused about what we are going to make as the final product. At first, we are still debating which fastener for the project.
Once we agree on the fastener, we meet another issue of coming up with the topic or theme as the final. Before, the final product is around the monster theme. It is an animal theme.
During the laser testing, we realized it is too difficult to put the pieces together and build an animal. Later on, we decided to change it to a monster theme.
After we all agree on the theme and fastener, we then build our five or six individual designs for the theme. Each of us has our unique shapes of head and body parts, big and small.
Everything seems fine and on the right track, but we still worry about the outcome and the model build-up. When it comes to the actual build day and going to the class in reality.
Surprisingly, the pieces are working fine, and we can build a "monster-like" creature from those pieces.

In the process of building the model, the fun part is it allows people to build the monster completely random and also base on any certain creatures.
For what it can be improved, is more designs for the shapes, also more other colors. When I am building it, even though it looks like there are varieties of shapes to pick from,
but they are not the best option to pick on. Another part is recording the video and documentation.
